Google forms

Google Forms makes creating forms of any kind from quizzes, to surveys, job application, and party invites, so easy. In this module, you will learn how to set one up from scratch and all the options you need to know to share them and get answers to your questions.

Gmail

The Gmail or Google Mail is a free email service introduced by Google. It allows sending and receiving mails over the Internet.This module teaches you how to send emails, manage your inbox, and perform other basic tasks in Gmail. .
